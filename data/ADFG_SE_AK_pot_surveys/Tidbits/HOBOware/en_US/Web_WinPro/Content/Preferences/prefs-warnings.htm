Warnings Preferences

Some warning dialogs offer you a checkbox to disable the warning so that you do not see it again. You can manage (and re-enable) these warnings in the Warnings Preferences pane.

Warnings are listed in three groups:

  • General
  • Warnings Before the Launch Screen
  • Warning After the Launch Screen

To enable all of the warnings in a group, click the Select All button for that group.

To disable all of the warnings in a group, click the Select None button for that group. Before you do so, however, keep in mind that the warnings are intended to help prevent mistakes and inadvertent data loss. Review each warning carefully before disabling it.
