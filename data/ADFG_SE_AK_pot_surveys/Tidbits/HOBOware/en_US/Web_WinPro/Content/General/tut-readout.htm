Reading Out the Logger

  1. Click the Readout icon on the toolbar.
  2. Click Stop when prompted if you want to stop the logger before reading out.

After reading out the logger, HOBOware prompts you to save the datafile.

  1. Click Save.

The Plot Setup dialog appears.

  1. Review the settings. The channels you enabled in the Launch window should be checked, along with button and other events (if applicable).
  2. Click Plot.
