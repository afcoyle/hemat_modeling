Select Channel to Configure Dialog

  1. Click the plus sign next to the module/sensor name to see available channels.
  2. Select the desired channel.
  3. Click on the button for the action you want to perform: Disable, Configure, Refresh.

If you click Configure, the Configure Channel dialog appears.
