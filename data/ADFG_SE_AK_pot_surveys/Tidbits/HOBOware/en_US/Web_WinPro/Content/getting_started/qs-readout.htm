Reading Out the Logger

  1. Click the Readout icon on the toolbar.
  2. Click Stop when HOBOware Pro asks if you want to stop the logger before reading out.

After reading out the logger, HOBOware Pro prompts you to save the datafile.

  1. Click Save. The Plot Setup dialog appears.
  2. Review the settings on the Plot Setup dialog. The channels you enabled in the Launch window should be checked, along with button and other events (if applicable).
  3. Click Plot.
